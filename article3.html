Introducing the Aqua Alert Pro
The Aqua Alert Pro is a revolutionary new smart bracelet designed for all those who love spending time at the beach. The Aqua Alert Pro is packed with helpful features including:
- Shark sensors accurate to two kilometers
- pH sensors to detect unsafe waters
- Blood pressure monitors to monitor bleeding
- Satellite phone technology to call emergency services
- 48 hour battery life per charge
- Heart rate monitor to track your physical activity
